How We Refurbish – Assessment to Reintroduction

Every unit starts with a structured assessment to determine the most sustainable next step—refurbishment when viable, or responsible recycling when it’s not.

  1. 1

    Detailed assessment

    Age and service history, structural integrity, compressor health, electrical components, refrigerant compliance, and a cost-versus-value analysis.

  2. 2

    Refurbish or recycle

    If refurbishment is viable, we restore the unit with certified parts and thorough testing; if not, we salvage usable components and responsibly recycle materials.

  3. 3

    Reintroduction to service

    Resale, distribution, or rentals—supported by documentation and optional impact reporting to help you evidence sustainability outcomes.
